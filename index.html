Student Clubs

The RV College of Engineering is abuzz with activities from a wide range of clubs that spans across all disciplines of engineering. Find yourself updated with each one of them here!

Ashwa Racing

The Ashwa Mobility team comprises of UG-engineers from RVCE, who design and build formula-style race cars. Ashwa Mobility Foundation has produced 15 prototype race cars including both the combustion and hybrid, which have participated annually in events held across America, Europe, Asia and Australia.

Astra Robotics

Started in 2015, Astra Robotics is involved in designing and developing 1. Astra Mars Rover 2. An autonomous car

Team Chimera

Started in 2006, the team is involved in designing and fabricating Formula styled Electric prototypes for FSAE events. Team Chimera has participated in various national and international events.
